Asiah's Data

Here is where I began my project. Over the course of 5 days I tracked where and what I listened to. The main point of the project was to see what I listened to while at the gym vs when im studying.

What i found is that I tend to listen to certain genres more when im studying vs when Im at the gym. For example, I listen to Pop more while im studying, and I listen to Rap more while im working out. What I also found by looking at my data vis, was that I tend to study more at night nad workout more in the morning My data vis shows my habits in a hopefully clear way. The Icons repsresent the different genres of usic i listen to, and the lines represent the five days that I tracked my music
