Asiah's Data

Here is where I began my project. Over the course of 5 days i tracked where and what i listened to. The main point of the project was to see what I listened to while at the gym vs when im studying.

What I found from this data is that i tend to study more at night, and go to the gym in the morning. I also found that I listen to pop music a lot more when I study, and rap or R&B while im at the gym. This came as a surprise to me because I like to think of myself as a hipster, and the amount of mainstream pop music I listen to disturbs me. I also saw that I actually listen to a wider ranger of genres whilst im studying, compared to when I'm at the gym I only listen to 1 or 2 genres.

Explained Video Section

This project was more researched based than the first. For this project I decided to look the Pandora algorithim that reccomends music to its listeners .This is known as the Pandora Music Genome. It is an actually very complex algorithim that gathers the types of music people listen to, and tries to reccomend genres, songs, or artists a person maight like based on what they like, or what they've listened to in the past. As you will be able to see in my explained video Pandora goes the extra mile to reccomend music that its listeners will actually want to listen to. The music genome, according to Pandoras CEO, is only just getting started. The compnay hopes to take music listening to another level, especially for its premium listeners.
